PhotoWalk

EVENT DESCRIPTION:

Photowalk is a photography event of the most awaited techno-cultural fest “MERAZ’19”. Capture your surroundings; pour out your imagination into your photographs along with a caption allowing the photograph to be the best photograph of the event. 

DURATION OF THE EVENT:

90 minutes

LOCATION:

IIT BHILAI, GEC Raipur Campus, Sejbahar, Raipur.

THEME OF THE EVENT:

This is an open theme contest.

RULES AND REGULATIONS OF PHOTOWALK:

1.)Only ONE Photo can be submitted for the competition.

2.)Participants should bring their own cameras . Cameras will not be provided.

Usage of tripod is allowed.

3.)The photos must be submitted via email to the email address “tps@iitbhilai.ac.in” with the subject PHOTOWALK and the body should contain participant’s name and contact details.

4.)Submissions will not be accepted once the deadline lapses..

5.)IIT BHILAI campus area is restricted for the photowalk.

6.)Submitted photo should contain captured time and the device name with which photo is captured.

7.)Photos should be sent in JPG/JPEG format. 

8.)Participants can capture the photo using different filters but 

NO EDITING is allowed on the submitted photo.

9.)Photos that portray or otherwise include inappropriate and/or offensive content, including provocative nudity, violence, human rights and/or environmental violation, and/or any other contents deemed to be contrary to the law, religious, cultural & moral traditions and practices of India, are strictly prohibited and will be immediately discarded.

10.)Participants disobeying the above mentioned rules will be disqualified.

11.) In case of any dispute, the decision of the organizing team shall be final. 

If you have any queries please feel free to contact any of the Event CoOrdinators.

NOTE:

Participants has to purchase MERAZ ENTRY PASS.

SUBMISSION DEADLINE:

90min after commencement of the event.

JUDGING CRITERIA : 

Technical excellence : 30%

Composition : 20%

Creativity & originality : 30% 

Visual impact : 20%

Registration Fee:

No Registration Fee

Prizes worth:

Rs. 8,000/-
